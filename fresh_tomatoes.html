C. Teal's Favorite Movies

The Sandlot

(1993)

A new kid in town is taken under the wing of a young baseball prodigy and his team in this coming of age movie set in the summer of 1962.

Mrs. Doubtfire

(1993)

After a bitter divorce, an actor disguises himself as a female housekeeper to spend time with his children held in custody by his former wife.

Wedding Crashers

(2005)

John Beckwith and Jeremy Grey, a pair of committed womanizers who sneak into weddings to take advantage of the romantic tinge in the air, find themselves at odds with one another when John meets and falls for Claire Cleary.

Days of Thunder

(1990)

A young hot-shot stock car driver gets his chance to compete at the top level.

The Boondock Saints

(1999)

Fraternal twins set out to rid Boston of the evil men operating there while being tracked down by an FBI agent.

Casino

(1995)

Greed, deception, money, power, and murder occur between two mobster best friends and a trophy wife over a gambling empire.
